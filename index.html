About Me

My name is Cathy and I am a transplant from Texas that moved to Boston on a whim in 2015 with my husband for change and adventure! I am always finding the silver lining, striving to learn new things, and working to deepen my knowledge in subjects I already know. Web development gives me the chance to do those things and provides the opportunity for continual growth and creativity that I was looking for in my new career.

Please check out my projects and feel free to reach out if you have any questions, want to work together or discuss an available opportunity, or just to say "hi"!
